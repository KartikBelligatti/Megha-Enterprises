About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have questions about our products, services, and solutions. Below are answers to some of the most common inquiries from our customers to help you make informed decisions.

Do you provide installation services for CCTV systems?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all CCTV systems, including Hikvision, CP Plus, and Dahua, ensuring optimal setup and operation.

What warranties do you offer for your products?

Our products come with manufacturer warranties. Specific warranty durations vary by product, such as Exide batteries and Hikvision security solutions.

Can I customize my solar panel setup?

Absolutely! We provide tailored solar solutions to meet your unique energy needs, including residential and commercial installations.

Do you offer after-sales support?

Yes, our dedicated customer support team is always available to assist you with troubleshooting, maintenance, and any queries.

What are the payment options available at your store?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and digital payments for your convenience.

Still have questions? Contact us or visit our store, and we’ll be happy to assist you further!
